About Santa Maria, Laguna

Santa Maria, officially the Municipality of Santa Maria (Tagalog: Bayan ng Santa Maria), is a 2nd class municipality in the province of Laguna, Philippines. According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 34,511 people.
